# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

French-speaking outdoor and endurance community members. Runners and athletes who train in Zone 2, value long-distance discipline, and look for community aligned with a "slow is fast" philosophy. They arrive on the site to understand who z2club is and whether it's their kind of place.

## Product Purpose

A landing site that earns trust and signals identity, so the right people self-select into the community. Success is recognition, not conversion pressure.

## Brand Personality

Quiet. Technical. Enduring.

References: Satisfy Running (radical minimalism, strong type), Ciele Athletics (sober technical identity), Soar Running (lifestyle without fuss).

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: hero metrics, gradient CTAs, bento feature grids
- Loud fitness branding: aggressive typography, neon accents, transformation-before-after energy

## Design Principles

1. **Slow is fast** — Zone 2 philosophy applied to design: restraint over noise, patience over urgency
2. **Earned credentials** — Technical credibility over hype; let the community speak for itself
3. **Economy of expression** — Every element justifies its presence; what's absent matters as much as what's there
4. **Outdoor intelligence** — Functional and readable in any condition, not precious
5. **Community over conversion** — Inform and attract, don't push; the right people will find their way in

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Sensible reduced-motion defaults.
